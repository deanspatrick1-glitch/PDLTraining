About Coach Patrick

Georgia-raised.
Hopkins-trained.

Patrick Deans grew up in North Atlanta and played at Lambert High School — one of the most competitive lacrosse programs in the state. From there, he earned a spot at Johns Hopkins University, where he played as a long-stick midfielder (LSM) for five years at one of the most storied programs in college lacrosse.

Now back in Atlanta, Patrick brings that D1 experience directly to the next generation of Georgia lacrosse players — through focused, high-quality individual and small group training.
